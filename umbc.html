About UMBC

The University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC) is located in Catonsville, Maryland. It is part of the University System of Maryland, which includes 12 institutions of higher education. In August 2022, Dr. Valerie Sheares Ashby was appointed as UMBC's president. Dr. Ashby is UMBC's first female president, replacing Dr. Freeman A. Hrabowski III after thirty years of service.

A Commitment to Sustainability

UMBC's Office of Sustainability was established in 2011. The Office of Sustainability reports out information on the University's greenhouse gas emissions, sustainabiity programs, and best practices. You can find out more about the office here.
